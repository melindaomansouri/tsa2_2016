Digital Media

Develop skills in graphics, animation, audio, video and game design. This course is offered as a full year or semester course. Digital media is the process of planning, instructional design, and development. Full year students will have the opportunity to choose a career emphasis and a possible internship in a design studio.

Instructor

Lisa Wazdeck has 15 years of teaching experience in Digital Media and Animation at CTEC. She received a Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts from the Utah State University and adds an artistic perspective to all things digital. In 2014, she won the CTEC's Teacher of the Year award.
